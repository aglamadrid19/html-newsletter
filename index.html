TRANSFORM THE WAY YOU SEE YOUR PATIENTS
 
A patient walks in complaining of chest pains... Imagine being able to use a simple handheld ultrasound device to improve your diagnosis process, thereby reducing the number of unneccessary referrals and improving patient outcomes.
 
This full-day workshop will help you to use portable hand-held scanning technology in routine diagnostics. The expert-led, practical and case-based training will include:
 
•  positioning the probe
 
•  knowing what normal looks like
 
•  recognising the abnormal
 
You'll cover urgent conditions such as chest complaints/shortness of breath and deep vein thrombosis, and non-urgent applications such as genitourinary problems and upper right quadrant pains.

London   Wednesday 12th October - Novotel London West
 
Leeds   Wednesday 19th October - Weetwood Hall
